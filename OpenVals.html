Why OpenVals?

Most AI models perform well in demonstrations, but production environments require something different. OpenVals evaluates beyond simple accuracy:

Trust & Reliability

Can the model be trusted and how does it behave under repeated execution?

Factuality & Hallucination

Is the response factually correct, and what is the estimated hallucination risk?

Dataset Integrity

Is the evaluation dataset itself validated and trustworthy?

Core Platform Capabilities

Decision Reliability Score (DRS)

A deployment-focused trust metric combining accuracy, semantic similarity, reliability, safety, consistency, variance, latency, factuality, and hallucination risk.

Factuality Engine

Dedicated engine performing semantic factual alignment, numeric consistency validation, contradiction detection, and risk classification.

Hallucination Probability Index (HPI)

Establishes the probability that a model response contains hallucinated or unreliable content (Low, Medium, High, Critical).

Dataset Intelligence

Ensures dataset schema, quality, duplicates, and missing fields are validated with the Dataset Health Score (DHS).

Parallel Execution Engine

Supports concurrent model execution to accelerate benchmarking runs significantly.

Executive Reporting

Generates executive-grade HTML dashboard reports (report.html) and sample-level reports (sample_report.html).

Quick Start

Install:

pip install openvals

Benchmark:

openvals benchmark --dataset finance --models mistral,llama3 --config finance

Validate Dataset:

openvals validate-dataset finance
